# Recipes Changed Since 1.7.5

This file lists recipe changes in 1.7.6 that may affect your setups or patterns.

**Note that this doesn't include new alternative recipes, new recipes, or deprecated recipes (where the original still exists, but a new, cheaper version has been added)**

## Both Modes

### Extended Crafting
**Large Widespread Breaking Change:**

Extended Crafting recipes are now 'strict'!    
This means that large table recipes (5x5, 7x7, 9x9) now MUST be performed in the table of the exact tier!    
E.g., you can no longer do Elite recipes in an Ultimate table!

### GregTech Cell Crafting, Bender, Extruder and Assembler Recipes
**Output Buff**

All outputs have been increased by 4x.

### ME Conduit Recipe
**Output Nerf**

Crafting recipe output amount nerfed from 4 to 1; assembler recipe output amount nerfed from 8 to 2. Use normal ME Glass Cables instead; only use ME Conduits when required.
